Teaching

My courses, teaching philosophy, and materials

I am deeply committed to teaching and continuously strive to enhance my pedagogical skills. I am a recipient of Harvard University's certificate in teaching, which included: 1) study and practice of pedagogical methods over the course of 3 semesters, 2) the development of my teaching statement, and 3) the creation of an original syllabus. In 2023, I also attended Bentley University's Strategies for Success in the College Classroom workshop to further develop my teaching style.
