Welcome! I am a PhD candidate in the Technology and Operations Management unit at Harvard Business School. I'm interested in the behavioral foundations that explain how people evaluate their prospects. I use microeconomic theory to model how decision makers form their beliefs and complement this with experimental methods to generate empirical support for my theories.

Prior to matriculating at Harvard, I worked in the Tech industry. I spent some time as a software developer at Southwest Airlines and as a product operations manager at Google. I received my undergrad in Management Information Systems from the University of Texas.
